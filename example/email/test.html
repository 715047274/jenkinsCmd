Audience

This communication is intended for all internal Dayforce employees.

What is the change and what does it mean for you?

We are pleased to announce the delivery of the expanded Q4 2024 Roadmap TV updates. Each quarter the Product Organization refines and refreshes the roadmaps. Here you will find the latest details on roadmap delivery for the next 6–12 months. This insight into the Product roadmap can assist you with your customer meetings and enquiries.

Value Proposition

Your knowledge of the product direction and future feature deliverables can give you confidence we are delivering the innovation and features our customers are expecting. We are committed to: 

‌

‌1. Continuous Planning: Our customers choose us to drive agility in their business and people. In turn, we must reserve the right to revisit our roadmap and plan on a continuous basis ensuring our efforts line up with our most critical priorities. 

‌

‌2. Quarterly Communication: We are committed to providing quarterly updates with rolling 12 to 18-month views across our Dayforce HCM portfolio. 

‌

‌3. Collaboration & Iteration: Our roadmap share outs should not only drive awareness into key initiatives, but also spark healthy dialogue internally about our focus areas and where we see opportunities to invest, innovate, and grow.

When?

The latest Roadmap TV videos and presentations for Q4 2024 are available now on SP&T Sharepoint.

Frequently asked questions

Question: Can I share this content with customers? 

‌Answer: This content is meant for an internal audience only. Our teams will work with Product Marketing and Enablement to produce and post public-facing artifacts to Highspot. 

‌

‌Question: If I don't see a specific feature on the roadmap, does that mean you're not working on it? 

‌Answer: If you don’t see a particular item in the quarterly content, reach out to the product contact and we can help provide clarity on its place in our backlog. 

‌

‌Question: How does this align with our SRP process? 

‌Answer: Product Management follows a continuous planning cycle with a quarterly checkpoint to refine the view of our roadmap delivery plans. Providing quarterly roadmap updates will allow us to calibrate our investments on a more continuous basis vs once a year. SRP is a company wide annual resource planning exercise.
